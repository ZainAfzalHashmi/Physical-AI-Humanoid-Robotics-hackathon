# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to ROS 2 and Humanoid Control Systems. This book is designed to empower beginners and intermediate learners to understand, build, and work with Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics systems through practical, real-world examples and projects.

## What You'll Learn

In this book, you'll explore the fundamental concepts of robotics, focusing on:

- **ROS 2 Core Concepts**: Understanding nodes, topics, services, and the publisher-subscriber pattern
- **Python Integration**: Bridging high-level AI agents with ROS controllers using rclpy
- **Robot Modeling**: Creating humanoid robot models using URDF and Xacro
- **Physical AI**: Applying AI concepts to physical robotic systems
- **Advanced Control**: Locomotion, manipulation, and balance for humanoid robots

## Target Audience

This book is designed for:
- Robotics engineers seeking to understand modern ROS 2 practices
- Advanced computer science students interested in robotics
- AI/ML practitioners looking to apply their skills to physical systems
- Researchers and developers working on humanoid robotics

## How to Use This Book

Each chapter follows a hands-on learning approach with:
- Clear theoretical explanations
- Practical code examples
- Hands-on exercises
- Real-world applications

Let's begin your journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics!
